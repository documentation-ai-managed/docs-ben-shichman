## Overview

Summit Trail provides expert fractional CTO services to empower your company with strategic technology leadership. You gain access to seasoned executives who guide growth, modernization, and scalable execution without the overhead of a full-time hire. Led by Ben Shichman, Summit Trail delivers tailored solutions for startups and enterprises facing complex tech challenges.

Whether you're scaling operations, migrating legacy systems, or building innovative products, Summit Trail aligns technology with your business goals. Engage on-demand expertise to accelerate decisions and reduce risks.
